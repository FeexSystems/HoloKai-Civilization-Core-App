### 6.1 Egyptian Cosmology - Maat
- maat: Truth, justice, balance, order. Depicted as woman with feather. Heart weighed against feather in Book of Dead (Chapter 125). 42 Negative Confessions.
- isfet: Chaos, lie, injustice. Pharaoh's job: maintain Maat against Isfet. Rituals, building temples, judging fairly.
- ba_ka_akh: Ka = life force, Ba = personality (bird), Akh = transformed spirit after rituals. Need all to live after death.
- book_of_dead: Actually Book of Coming Forth by Day, 200 chapters, spells for afterlife navigation.

### 6.2 Dogon Cosmology - Advanced Astronomy
- amma: Creator god, created earth by mixing with star.
- nommo: Twins, half human half fish, water spirits, sacrificed and resurrected, taught humans speech, weaving, smithing. Word = water = life.
- sirius: Dogon knew Sirius triple system: Sigi Tolo (Sirius A), Po Tolo (Sirius B white dwarf 50-year orbit invisible), Emme Ya Tolo (Sirius C). Known centuries before telescopes (1930s discovery of Sirius B). How? Debated.
- granary: Dogon granary architecture encodes cosmology - square base = earth, circular top = sky, steps = stages of creation.

### 6.3 Yoruba Ifa - Binary Computer
- ifa: Divination system, 256 Odu Ifa (2^8 = 256). Each Odu has 800+ verses (ese), total 200k verses memorized. UNESCO Intangible Heritage.
- binary: Each Odu marked by single and double lines (I and II) = 8 bits. Same as computer binary. Babalawo priest uses palm nuts and divination tray (opon Ifa).
- content: Verses include history, medicine, proverbs, law, ethics. Used to choose kings, cure, resolve disputes.
- orisha: 400+ orishas (spirits): Orunmila (wisdom), Ogun (iron, war, technology), Oshun (love, rivers), Shango (thunder - FEEXARA thunder code!), Yemaya (sea).
- diaspora: Spread to Cuba (Santeria), Brazil (Candomble), Haiti (Vodou).

### 6.4 Kongo Cosmology - Dikenga
- dikenga: Kongo cross, four moments of sun: birth (east), life (north), death (west), rebirth (south). Circle with cross inside.
- kalunga: Watery boundary between living and dead, white color = dead, black = living.
- nkisi: Spirits in objects, nkisi nkondi nail fetish - each nail is oath, contract. Early contract law.

### 6.5 Bantu Ubuntu & Secret Societies
- ubuntu: Nguni proverb Umuntu ngumuntu ngabantu - person is person through other people. Personhood relational not individual. Desmond Tutu popularized.
- secret_societies: Porro/Sande (Sierra Leone/Liberia) - control initiation, law, education. Sande is female society, Porro male. Ekpe (Nigeria/Cameroon) - leopard society, uses Nsibidi script, controls trade, justice. Ogboni (Yoruba) - earth elders, judicial.
- griot: West African historian, musician, advisor. Role: blood of society, keep genealogy, mediate. Instruments: kora (21 strings), balafon.

### 6.6 Ancestor Veneration - OS
- concept: Dead are living-dead, not gone, still influence. Land belongs to ancestors. King mediates between ancestors and living.
- shrines: Family shrine, village shrine, national shrine. Libation: pour drink for ancestors.
- zulu: Amadlozi ancestors, sangoma diviner.
