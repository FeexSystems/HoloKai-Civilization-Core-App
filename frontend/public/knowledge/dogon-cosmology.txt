DOGON COSMOLOGY (Mali, West Africa)

The Dogon possess one of the most sophisticated traditional cosmologies known. Their knowledge of the Sirius star system without telescopes has fascinated astronomers.

KEY KNOWLEDGE:
- Sirius B (Po Tolo): described a dense invisible companion star before 1862 confirmation
- The Nommo: amphibious beings from Sirius who brought civilization
- Orion alignment corresponds with Giza pyramid alignment
- Dogon calendar based on Sirius 50-year cycle matching Sirius B orbit
- Understanding of moon rotation, planets, and galactic structure

CONCEPTS:
- Universe created from a single vibration (parallels Big Bang theory)
- Spiral as fundamental shape of creation
- Knowledge encoded in masks, dances, and rituals
- Sigui ceremony every 60 years marks Sirius cycle
