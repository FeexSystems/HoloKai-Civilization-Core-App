ADINKRA SYMBOLS (Akan, Ghana)

Adinkra are visual symbols from the Akan people encoding proverbs and philosophy.

KEY SYMBOLS:
- Sankofa: learn from the past to build the future
- Gye Nyame: supremacy of the divine
- Dwennimmen: strength in humility
- Nkonsonkonson: unity and community
- Nyame Nti: faith
- Ananse Ntontan: wisdom and creativity

SIGNIFICANCE:
- Each symbol compresses complex ideas into graphic form
- Used in textiles, architecture, pottery, jewelry
- Adinkra cloth worn for funerals and special occasions
- Globally adopted as representations of African heritage
