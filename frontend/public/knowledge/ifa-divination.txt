IFA DIVINATION (Yoruba, West Africa)

Ifa is a divination system and philosophy of the Yoruba people. UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.

THE SYSTEM:
- 16 principal odu, 256 combinations, thousands of verses
- Each odu encodes history, medicine, ethics, prophecy
- Babalawo priests trained for years to interpret
- Uses palm nuts, divination chain (opele), or board (opon Ifa)

PHILOSOPHICAL DEPTH:
- Concept of Ase (divine energy)
- Balance, destiny (ayanmo), character (iwa pele)
- Thousands of proverbs and poems embedded in verses

SCIENTIFIC PARALLELS:
- Binary structure (16 x 16 = 256) mirrors computer code
- Predates Leibniz's binary system by centuries

KEY FIGURES:
- Orunmila — orisha of wisdom
- Chief FAMA, Wande Abimbola — modern interpreters
